You are an elite UI/UX Designer and a Senior Full-Stack Developer. Your task is to build a beautiful, high-converting, end-to-end web application for "SCAVA" — a premium specialty café and pickleball court community based in Nellore. 

The website must act as both a visual brand statement and an operational tool to drive customer acquisition, court bookings, and community engagement.

### 1. BRAND IDENTITY & VISUAL AESTHETICS (CRITICAL)
SCAVA's identity is defined by "Scandinavian-inspired simplicity" meets "high-energy lifestyle." You must implement the following design tokens strictly:
- **Design Philosophy:** Minimalist, intentional, structured, and premium. Lots of whitespace, crisp typography, and high-contrast layouts.
- **Color Palette:** - Dominant: Deep charcoal/matte black and crisp architectural whites.
  - Accents: Warm earth tones (latte, espresso browns) and a sharp, muted energetic tone (like a refined pickleball neon/volt or court green used very sparingly for CTA highlights).
- **Typography:** Bold, clean sans-serif headings (e.g., Inter, Syne, or Clash Display vibes) with highly readable, spacious body text.
- **Atmosphere:** Evoke a "pause button for the chaos." Use smooth, organic fade-in animations and transitions that mimic a premium lifestyle portfolio rather than a generic template.

### 2. CORE PAGES & STRUCTURE
- **Home Page:**
  - **Hero Section:** High-impact bold typography stating "More than coffee. More than a court." or "Brew hard. Play harder." Split layout or overlapping visual blocks showcasing the dual nature of the space (Specialty Drip Coffee & Fast Rallies).
  - **About / The Vibe:** Elegant text layout explaining the Scandinavian design, the community, and the ritual of slow-brewed coffee.
  - **Feature Split:** Two prominent, beautifully designed entry points: "The Court" (Pickleball) and "The Brew" (Café Menu teaser featuring items like the Malli Kaalan Vada).
  - **Social Proof / Instagram Grid:** A clean, curated mockup section displaying community moments.
- **The Court (Pickleball Experience):**
  - Informational section explaining why pickleball is the fastest-growing social sport.
  - Interactive court scheduler/booking widget framework.
- **The Brew & Bite (Interactive Menu):**
  - A gorgeous, minimalist menu grid highlighting specialty drip coffee, slow roasts, and signature items (like the spicy mushroom Malli Kaalan Vada). Include filter tabs for "Coffee Rituals" and "Bites".

### 3. CONVERSION & FUNCTIONAL FEATURES
- **Court Booking Engine:** Create an intuitive, step-by-step reservation system where users can select a date, time slot, and number of players. Include a clean summary breakdown before a mock checkout.
- **"Show Up" Call-to-Actions (CTAs):** High-contrast sticky navigation buttons for "Book a Court" or "Find Us".
- **Location & Hours Widget:** Dedicated, high-end map integration placeholder and operational hours tailored for Nellore locals.
- **Contact & Events Panel:** A sleek form for private court bookings, corporate events, or community tournaments.

### 4. TECHNICAL & ARCHITECTURAL REQUIREMENTS
- **Tech Stack:** Build this using a modern, fast full-stack architecture (e.g., Next.js/React with Tailwind CSS, or Vite + Python/Node backend as per Replit's optimal native setup).
- **Responsiveness:** Flawless mobile-first design. The mobile viewport must feel like a premium native lifestyle app.
- **State Management:** Fully functional front-end state for the booking flows, menu sorting, and contact form submissions. Use a local Replit database to persist mock court bookings so they don't disappear on refresh.
- **Clean Code:** Componentize the code heavily (e.g., `Hero.jsx`, `BookingCalendar.jsx`, `MenuGrid.jsx`). Use Tailwind utility classes intelligently to maintain the strict minimalist design system.

Start by outlining the component structure, validating the design tokens, and then proceed to build the full application step-by-step.
